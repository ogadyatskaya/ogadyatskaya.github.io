Interest in research projects

I am interested in participating in research projects, especially HorizonEurope (Clusters 3&4) and NWO, on topics relevant to my research. I am experienced in writing research proposals for various funding agencies (EC, NWO, FNR). If you see any opportunities for collaboration, please contact me via email.

Past selected research projects

  • DroidMod (2016-2020, FNR, Luxembourg): I was the proposer of this Luxembourgish research project. The project funded 1 PhD student on mobile security research.
  • COMMA (2016-2019, FNR, Luxembourg): I was the proposer and the PI of this Luxembourgish research project. COMMA was dedicated to developing new solutions for mobile malware detection using program analysis.
  • TREsPASS (2013-2017, EU FP7): I was acting as the University of Luxembourg representative in this large EU project focused on automating cyber risk assessment for socio-technical systems.
  • SecCord (2013-2016, EU FP7): I was acting as a WP leader in this CSA project dedicated to supporting EU FP7 security projects.
